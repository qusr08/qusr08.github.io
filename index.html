Breakthrough

May 2022 - Present

Breakthrough is an arcade game where the player must use boom blocks to blow up other tiles. The goal is to get a piece under player's control all the way to the bottom of the game board, 'breaking through' the level. The game is endless and each level gets progressively harder to clear.
