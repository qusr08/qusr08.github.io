About Me

Hello! I'm Frankie and I love to make games! I have been programming since 2013 and have been fascinated with video games ever since I could hold a controller. Growing up, I have competed in multiple robotics teams throughout my middle/high school years.

I have experience programming in many languages including C#, C, C++, Python, Java, and Javascript, My favorite language is C# because I enjoy and have experience developing games in Unity.

Currently I am a third-year student at the Rochester Institute of Technology studying Game Design and Development. I am using my time here to develop my game designing skills and to build a portfolio I am proud of.

My Interests

Puzzle games are my favorite! I have always loved spending time analyzing puzzles and the problem-solving aspect that comes with them. Designing puzzle mechanics and complex interactions in games is very intriguing to me. I feel many of my portolio pieces relfect this interest as well.

I am also very interested in randomness and procedural generation in games. Randomness is a great way to infinitely expand upon any game idea and make each gameplay experience unique from the last.

My favorite aspects of game development are gameplay programming and level design. Over the few years I have been at college, I have also grown an interest for web development and UI design.

Outside of technology, I find space fascinating and love learning about how the universe works. I also like to take walks or ride my bike when I can to stay active.
