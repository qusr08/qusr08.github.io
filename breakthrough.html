Game Description

Breakthrough is a gravity block puzzle game inspired by games similar to Tetris. The player's goal is to score as many points as possible by clearing boards using various Minos and Boom Blocks. Each Mino is a shape made up of six blocks which may have a Boom Block attached to it when created. When a Mino is placed onto the board, Boom Blocks are triggered and explode the surrounding area in various patterns. The player's goal is to break through the generated wall on the board and reach the bottom, achieving a "breakthrough". The boards will also automatically increase in difficulty the longer the player is in the game.

My Thoughts

This game won the Game Feel award at the 2023 Game Showcase and Expo at RIT and also was funded by the Magic Maker Program at RIT over the summer of 2023. I worked on this game with my partner Gabriela Colón-Meléndez, where I was responsible for all of the programming and she led the visual design. We are still actively working on the game, with our end goal for this game being to release it on Steam.

I am beyond excited about this project and very proud of how far we have come in the development process. We are motivated to finish Breakthrough to the best of our abilities and want to see the game succeed.
